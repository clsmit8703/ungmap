About UngMap

UNGmap is a website to help students at the University of North Georgia find buildings, parking lots, recreation areas, and faculty members. We wanted to make this helpful website since UNG has gone through some huge changes over the last couple of years. In 2013, Gainesville State College and North Georgia College & State University merged into the University of North Georgia. Because of the merge, several buildings had their names changed, students began going to more than one campus, and parking lots were either being built or shutdown. We hope students will use this website to figure how to navigate all four UNG campuses.
